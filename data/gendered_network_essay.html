GENDERED NETWORKS ↓

This network graph draws on personal information from the individuals in Ocampo’s network – both biological data (sex) and biographical data (occupation) – in an effort to showcase the relationships among these people on a larger scale. The vibrantly colored nodes here pertain to an individual’s sex – male (blue) or female (pink) –, while the grey nodes show their shared occupations.
